This is my first post so my page isn't completely empty. My intention is to build this site into a webfolio/blog for myself, by combining content that spans my diverse interests I hope to keep myself motivated. On that note I will introduce myself, I'm Jordy a 31 year old Australian, I've been scaffolding the last 8 years including while I studied my bachelors of Design. While persuing my studies at UNSW I realised my passion lay in development while taking elective CompSci courses.

Since graduating at the end of 2022 I halfheartedly continued learning in my spare time. I agreed to remain scaffolding for the next 6 months in exchange for some work perks as we were very short-staffed. Since then I've been applying to junior and graduate dev roles hoping to finally transition from construction. That brings me to now, after growing frustrated at my slow progress and lack of much to show I decided to create this webfolio, as a standalone project and a blog dump for my other various interests.
